The sanctuary veil is drawn open.
The deacon enters the santaury and incenses,
proceeding from right to left.
At the entrance of the sanctuary, he incenses the people.
It is fitting to incense up to the main door of the church.

In the Simple Form, the second stanza may be omitted.
